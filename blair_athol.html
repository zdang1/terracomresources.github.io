Queensland’s Flagship Thermal Coal Operation

Blair Athol Mine

Delivering high-quality thermal coal since reopening in 2017

  • Annual Production
  • Extended Mine Life
  • Strategic Acquisition & Rehabilitation

TerraCom acquired Blair Athol in 2017 and successfully restarted production in Clermont, Queensland 

Currently exporting approximately 2.2 million tonnes per annum of high‑quality thermal coal 

A 2021 JORC update increased marketable reserves to ~19.5 million tonnes, extending the mine’s life by ~10 years to around 2035 

Purchased from Rio Tinto (for A$1) with rehabilitation provisions (~A$79.6 m), and 50 ha of progressive site rehabilitation initiated in 2017 

Operates as a large-scale open pit using truck & shovel and dragline systems, with on-site processing and rail logistics 

Produces thermal coal for export to Japan, South Korea, and India’s sponge iron industry; positioned as a dual processing hub with the upcoming Moorlands integration
