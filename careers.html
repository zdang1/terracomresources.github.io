Empowering Sustainable Mining for a Global Future

We operate across Australia and South Africa, delivering reliable energy through responsible mining practices. At TerraCom, our focus is on long-term value, environmental stewardship, and building strong community partnerships.

Our Core Operations

Australian Assets

Efficient, high-yield coal production from flagship sites like Blair Athol and Springsure Creek. Focused on delivering value through innovation and operational excellence.

South African Operations

Diverse mining assets including Kangala and New Clydesdale, supplying premium-grade thermal and metallurgical coal to global markets.

Exploration & Expansion

Strategic development of future mining zones to ensure a stable, scalable resource pipeline aligned with sustainable practices.

Key Aspects of Our Operation

Resource Assessment

We evaluate geological data and resource potential across our tenements to ensure viable, long-term mining operations.

Mine Development

Our expert teams plan and construct efficient mine sites, prioritising safety, environmental impact, and productivity from day one.

Operational Excellence

With a focus on low-cost, high-output mining, we optimise processes and apply innovative technologies across all active sites.

Export & Logistics

We manage secure, scalable logistics to deliver coal to international markets via rail, port, and shipping infrastructure.

Sustainability & Community

We are committed to responsible environmental management, Indigenous engagement, and long-term benefits for local communities.

Delivering Capability and Expertise

TerraCom operates high-performing coal assets across Australia and South Africa. With a focus on innovation, efficiency, and sustainability, we deliver reliable energy and long-term value through every stage of the mining lifecycle.
