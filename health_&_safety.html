Health & Safety

The health and safety of our people is a key driver for us, and we are committed to providing a safe working environment, while ensuring we achieve our production targets.

Our objective is simple – our employees and contractors are empowered to consider safety in everything they do. Strong, visible leadership; positive role models; simple and effective systems and total commitment from our people are what make our workplaces safe.

Safety is a mindset and an attitude we expect from everyone; it must be considered a core value that is instilled in all parts of our organisation, part of our overall business culture and foremost in everything we do.
