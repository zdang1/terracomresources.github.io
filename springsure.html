High-Quality Thermal and PCI Coal Opportunity

Springsure Project

Located in the Central‑Western Bowen Basin, shortlisted for development by TerraCom

  • Strategic Location
  • Extensive Tenure
  • Large Resource Base
  • Ownership Structure
  • Development Potential
  • Premium Coal Quality

Situated 8 km north of Springsure in Queensland’s Bowen Basin with access to the Gregory Highway and a rail spur to Gladstone Port (~420 km).

Covers EPC 1674, EPC 1103, and MDL 3002, spanning approximately 1,178 square kilometres of coal-rich terrain.

Holds a JORC-compliant thermal and PCI coal resource of approximately 191.5 million tonnes (148 Mt Inferred, 43 Mt Indicated).

TerraCom holds an 87% interest in the project via its subsidiary, Springsure Mining Pty Ltd.

Planned for underground mining with potential for future open-cut, backed by promising geology and proximity to infrastructure.

Targets high-energy, low-ash coal within the Reids Dome Beds, similar to neighbouring Minerva and Rolleston mines.
