Strategic Thermal Coal Tenement with Expansion & Integration Potential

Clydesdale Coal Project

TerraCom acquired 100% of Universal Coal Plc (Universal) as at 30 June 2020. Universal holds a portfolio of producing, development and exploration assets located across South Africa’s major coalfields.

  • Portfolio Integration
  • Tenure & Resource Block
  • Operational Proximity
  • Mining Potential
  • Infrastructure Support
  • Strategic Growth Opportunity

Part of TerraCom’s broader South African coal portfolio, fully integrated with Kangala and Eloff operations to consolidate regional asset value

Occupies EPC licenses in the Witbank Coalfield region—aligned with New Clydesdale Colliery’ exploration rights spanning ~15,000 ha 

Located near Kangala pit and Eloff block, enabling shared use of processing plants, rail loadouts, and power stations

Universal Coal’s assets suggest strong continuity—New Clydesdale is delivering ~2.7 Mtpa, supporting ongoing operations and demonstrating expandable life-of-mine potential 

Eloff project directly contiguous (424 Mt SAMREC resource), enabling low-cost, near-term extension of Kangala Complex—Clydesdale serves as the groundwork for potential standalone growth 

Positioned to future-proof TerraCom’s South African presence—supporting next-stage development through integrated processing, logistics, and resource consolidation
