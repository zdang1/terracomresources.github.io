High-Energy Thermal Coal Resource with Export Potential

Clyde Park Coal Project

Exploring Queensland’s Galilee Basin for high-potential thermal coal resources with strategic infrastructure access and long-term development value.

  • Strategic Location

TerraCom holds a 64.4% stake in Clyde Park Coal Pty Ltd, with the remainder held by Tiaro Coal Limited (ASX: TCM) 

The project covers EPC 1250 and EPC 1260 on the northeastern edge of the Galilee Basin, Queensland, spanning approximately 133 km² 

Historic JORC Inferred resource estimates reached ~623 Mt of thermal coal, with ongoing drilling targeting additional upgrades 

Drilling has identified multiple Betts Creek Beds seams; resource estimations capped at ~300 m depth to support future open‑cut development 

Located ~15 km from the Mt Isa–Townsville rail line near Pentland, offering strong future logistics and port connectivity 

Quality modelling indicates the potential for moderate-ash export thermal coal, subject to further testing and washability analysis
