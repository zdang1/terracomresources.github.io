Northern Galilee Exploration

TerraCom holds expansive exploration tenements across the Northern Galilee Basin, targeting untapped coal resources in regions like Hughenden, Pentland, and Sierra.

Hughenden & Pentland Projects

Strategically positioned in the northern Galilee Basin of Queensland, the Hughenden and Pentland projects span roughly 2,500 km² of contiguous exploration ground. Hughenden is the most advanced, with an Indicated resource of ~133 Mt and an additional 1.1 Bt Inferred, while neighbouring Pentland adds thousands more square kilometres of highly prospective coal seams just 25 km west of the township of Pentland and close to existing rail corridors that link to the Port of Townsville. Together, these assets form TerraCom’s long-term growth engine—offering large-scale, low-strip-ratio thermal coal potential, optional pathways to export markets, and the flexibility to phase development responsibly as infrastructure and demand evolve

Sierra & Kolan Prospects

Sierra & Kolan Prospects – Exploration for Tomorrow
TerraCom’s Sierra and Kolan prospects sit at opposite ends of Queensland yet share the same strategic purpose: unlocking the next wave of coal growth.

Sierra Project – Bowen Basin
Granted exploration tenements in the northern Bowen Basin give Sierra access to world-class coal measures. Initial drilling programs are planned to delineate shallow, low-strip thermal and PCI coal seams, ideally positioned near established rail corridors leading to export ports.
TerraCom Limited

Kolan Project – Maryborough Basin
Just 20 km north-west of Bundaberg, Kolan covers roughly 237 km² over the Burrum Coal Measures. Historic studies point to hard-coking-coal potential of up to 100 Mt with CSN values of 7-9 after beneficiation. Proximity to Gladstone’s infrastructure offers a clear pathway from resource to market.

Future Growth Pipeline – TerraCom’s Next Wave of Value

TerraCom’s growth engine is already in motion. Beyond our producing mines we’re progressing a balanced pipeline of near-term and long-life assets:

Northern Galilee Hub – Hughenden & Pentland: large-scale, low-strip projects that anchor our Queensland expansion plans.

Bowen & Maryborough Prospects – Sierra & Kolan: early-stage tenements targeting premium PCI, thermal and coking coal, close to existing rail and port corridors.

Organic brownfield options: life-of-mine extensions at Blair Athol, productivity uplifts at Springsure and Clydesdale, plus debottlenecking across our South African operations.

Selective acquisitions: an agile balance-sheet and experienced team ready to add value-accretive assets that fit our low-cost, high-margin profile.

Together, these projects give TerraCom the flexibility to phase new production responsibly, diversify product mix, and keep delivering sustainable returns for investors well into the next decade.
