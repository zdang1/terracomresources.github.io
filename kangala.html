Strategic South African Coal Asset with Expansion-Ready Potential

Kangala Coal Mine

Part of TerraCom’s integrated portfolio, Kangala delivers consistent production with scalable development through adjacent resource opportunities.

  • Fully Acquired Asset
  • Strategic Portfolios
  • Complementary Assets
  • Infrastructure-Enabled Location
  • Scalable Extensions
  • Long-Term Growth Potential

TerraCom holds 100% of the Kangala Coal Mine through its acquisition of Universal Coal Plc in June 2020 

Located within the Wolvenfontein complex, adjacent to Kangala pit and the Eloff Project, offering near-term expansion flexibility 

Part of the Universal Coal portfolio which also includes Middelbult, New Clydesdale Colliery, Ubuntu, Eloff, and Arnot South 

Situated in one of South Africa’s major coalfields, with proximity to power stations and access to road, rail, and power infrastructure 

The adjacent Eloff Project provides potential for low-cost mine life expansion and standalone development within the Kangala area 

Embedded within a diversified coal assets network—including Ubuntu, Arnot South, and Berenice/Cygnus—supporting future development avenues
