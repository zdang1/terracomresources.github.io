About Us

At TerraCom Resources, we are a dynamic, global mining company committed to delivering sustainable returns for our shareholders, partners, and the communities we serve. Recognised for our reliability and integrity, we operate across Australia and South Africa with a focus on long-term value creation in the resources and energy sector.

Our purpose is clear: to responsibly manage and develop high-quality assets by leveraging innovation, strategic relationships, and a people-first approach. We believe success is built on a foundation of trust, collaboration, and respect—values that are reflected in our inclusive culture and strong industry reputation.

Driven by performance and passion, we are constantly finding new ways to lead in sustainable mining. Our operations prioritise environmental stewardship, safety, and operational excellence. We take pride in being accountable to all stakeholders while creating positive impact through meaningful partnerships and proactive community engagement.

By investing in our people and aligning with forward-thinking alliances, TerraCom continues to shape a resilient and future-ready resources industry—one that delivers consistent results today while preparing for the challenges of tomorrow.

Delivering Capability and Expertise

TerraCom operates high-performing coal assets across Australia and South Africa. With a focus on innovation, efficiency, and sustainability, we deliver reliable energy and long-term value through every stage of the mining lifecycle.

Danny McCarthy

Managing Director

Danny McCarthy is a seasoned mining executive with over 26 years of experience across Australia and international markets. Before joining TerraCom in 2018, he served as Chief Operating Officer at Mineral Resources Limited (ASX: MIN), where he oversaw major growth initiatives, including the development of the world-class Wodgina lithium mine.

Danny brings strong commercial and operational expertise to TerraCom, with a focus on strategy, mining, construction, and mineral processing. As CEO and now Managing Director, he successfully transitioned Blair Athol to an owner-operator model and led significant operational improvements in South Africa, driving long-term value and increased profitability.

Megan Etcell

Chief Financial Officer and Company Secretary

Megan Etcell is a Chartered Accountant with a Bachelor of Commerce from the University of Newcastle. She joined TerraCom in 2019 as Company Secretary and later took on the role of Executive General Manager Corporate Affairs, overseeing governance, investor relations, and regulatory matters. In 2021, she was appointed Interim CFO.

Megan brings extensive experience from the coal sector, having held senior roles at NuCoal Resources (ASX: NCR), where she was involved in capital raisings, acquisitions, and joint ventures.

FAQ

Where does TerraCom operate?

TerraCom operates coal mining assets in Australia and South Africa, including flagship sites like Blair Athol in Queensland and Kangala in South Africa.

What types of coal does TerraCom produce?

We produce a range of thermal and metallurgical coal products, serving both domestic and international energy markets.

How can I invest in TerraCom?

TerraCom is publicly listed on the ASX under the ticker TER. You can invest through any licensed stockbroker or trading platform.

What is TerraCom’s approach to sustainability?

Sustainability is core to our operations. We focus on responsible resource management, environmental protection, and proactive community engagement in all regions where we operate.

How does TerraCom support local communities?

We engage directly with local communities through employment, training, partnerships, and ongoing support initiatives designed to deliver long-term benefits.

Where can I access company reports and updates?

You can find investor announcements, financial reports, and quarterly updates in the Investor Centre section of our website.
